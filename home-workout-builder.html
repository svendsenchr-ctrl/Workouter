Step 8 of 8
How should it progress?
Progression means the program gets harder each week — more rounds, higher reps, longer holds, and harder exercise variants.
Dynamic Progression
Reps, holds, and rounds increase automatically every week. Variants step up mid-program. Adjust each week via the review prompt.
Fixed difficulty
Same exercises, reps, and rounds every week. Good if you want consistency or are in a busy period.
